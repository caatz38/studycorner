About StudyCorner

Our Story

StudyCorner (formerly MindShare) was born out of frustration. As IB students, we were constantly overwhelmed by scattered notes, unclear explanations, too many links, and a lack of quality practice questions. We knew there had to be a better way. So we built it. StudyCorner is a platform where students help other students organizing notes, sharing teacher materials, and creating practice questions all in one simple, accessible place.
