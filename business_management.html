How to Answer Case Study Questions
‚ñº

Learn the PEEL method (Point, Evidence, Explain, Link) to structure your answers effectively. Always reference the case study in your response and apply business theory to real scenarios.

Command Terms Explained
‚ñº
  • Define - Give exact meaning ‚Üí
  • Explain - Show cause and effect ‚Üí
  • Evaluate - Weigh pros and cons ‚Üí

Time Management in Exams
‚ñº

Allocate 1.5 minutes per mark. For a 10-mark question, spend 15 minutes. Always leave 10 minutes at the end for review and checking your work.
